ðŸŽ¨ Color Psychology & Justification

Deep Teal/Sage (#4A6B6B): Evokes tranquility, balance, and natural luxury. This calming color represents the peaceful moonlit nights and premium spa experiences. It builds trust and sophistication.

Cream/Beige (#E8DCC4): Warm and elegant, this color adds a touch of organic luxury. It represents natural soy wax and creates a comfortable, inviting atmosphere that feels premium yet approachable.

Soft Gold (#C9A961): Symbolizes luxury, prestige, and quality. This accent color makes call-to-action buttons feel exclusive and valuable, encouraging purchases while maintaining elegance.

Deep Navy/Charcoal (#2C3E50): Provides authority and professionalism. This text color ensures excellent readability while conveying trustworthiness and modern sophistication.

This palette creates a cohesive luxury experience that appeals to our target audience of home decorators and gift buyers seeking premium, artisanal products.
